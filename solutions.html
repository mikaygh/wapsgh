Broadcast Solutions and Services

Premium DTH Satellite Broadcast Solutions for West Africa

In today's fast-evolving media landscape, satellite broadcast services are vital for delivering high-quality content to audiences worldwide. Our company provides a fully managed Direct-to-Home (DTH) headend infrastructure, ensuring seamless content distribution across various African markets.

Our end-to-end solution covers everything from satellite uplink and downlink to encoding and multiplexing, guaranteeing superior transmission quality.

Designed to support a diverse range of programming—including live events, news, sports, and entertainment our advanced DTH headend infrastructure offers unmatched reliability and efficiency. Backed by a state-of-the-art facility and a team of skilled engineers and technicians, we provide broadcasters, content providers, and satellite operators with a cost-effective and scalable media distribution solution.

Beyond content distribution, we also offer satellite transponder leasing and capacity management services, ensuring uninterrupted content availability—even in remote regions. Our commitment is to seamless broadcasting, high-quality content delivery, and exceptional support, allowing you to focus on engaging your audience while we handle the technical complexities.

K-NET Teleport

Channel Uplink

We provide reliable and secure channel uplink services to ensure seamless transmission of TV channels to satellites. Our state-of-the-art teleport facilities support C-band and Ku-band uplinks, offering extensive coverage for global and regional broadcasting. Whether for free-to-air (FTA) or encrypted channels, our robust uplink solutions guarantee high availability, minimal latency, and superior signal quality.

Remote Playout

Our remote playout services enable broadcasters to manage their channels from anywhere in the world. We offer fully automated and cloud-based playout solutions that provide:
• 24/7 monitoring & scheduling
• Dynamic content management
• Seamless integration with automation systems
• With our low-latency playout infrastructure, we ensure uninterrupted content delivery while reducing operational costs and complexities.

Transcoding

To ensure compatibility across various broadcasting and streaming platforms, our advanced transcoding solutions convert media into multiple formats, resolutions, and bitrates. This enables: Efficient bandwidth management Multi-platform content delivery (SD, HD, UHD) Optimized video quality for satellite, OTT, IPTV, and mobile networks Our real-time transcoding solutions support MPEG-2, MPEG-4, H.264, H.265 (HEVC), and AV1, ensuring exceptional video compression and minimal data loss.

IP Contribution

Reliable, low-latency video transport with error correction and encryption for live broadcasting. Microwave Contribution – High-capacity wireless transmission for point-to-point and point-to-multipoint connectivity, ideal for remote production and live events. With these solutions, we empower broadcasters to deliver high-quality content efficiently and cost-effectively, ensuring a superior viewing experience for audiences worldwide.
