Broadcast Solutions and Services

Comprehensive, Seamless Content Delivery

In today's fast-evolving media landscape, satellite broadcast services are vital for delivering high-quality content to audiences worldwide. Our company provides a fully managed Direct-to-Home (DTH) headend infrastructure, ensuring seamless content distribution across West and Central African markets.

Our end-to-end solution covers everything from satellite uplink and downlink to encoding and multiplexing, guaranteeing superior transmission quality. The broadcasting process begins with content contribution through various means such as IP, microwave, or fiber. The received signal then undergoes essential processing, including re-encoding, multiplexing (MUX), modulation, and encryption, along with additional DVB services like content management and playout. Once prepared, the signal is uplinked to the ASTRA 2F satellite for wide-area distribution. Finally, the transmitted signal reaches DTH households, ensuring seamless content delivery to viewers.

Designed to support a diverse range of programming—including live events, news, sports, and entertainment our advanced DTH headend infrastructure offers unmatched reliability and efficiency. Backed by a state-of-the-art facility and a team of skilled engineers and technicians, we provide broadcasters and content providers with a cost-effective and scalable media distribution solution.
Beyond content distribution, we also offer remote playout service and Media Asset Management System.

Free-To-Air Electronic Programme Guide

Our complimentary Electronic Programme Guide (EPG) for Free-to-Air (FTA) broadcasting plays a vital role in enhancing your channel's visibility, improving audience engagement, and increasing advertiser interest. EPGs serve as digital on-screen guides that provide viewers with program schedules, descriptions, and other key details, making it easier for them to discover and navigate content.

A well-structured EPG enhances viewer engagement by making content easily discoverable, giving your FTA channel a competitive edge. It also increases advertiser confidence by providing clear audience metrics, making the channel more attractive, accessible, and commercially viable for long-term success.




Free-To-Air Logical Channel Numbering System

Our complimentary Free-To-Air (FTA) Logical Channel Numbering (LCN) system enhances viewer experience by organizing channels in a clear, structured manner, making it easier for audiences to find and access their favorite content. By assigning logical numbers to different types of channels, such as news, entertainment, and sports, LCN simplifies navigation and ensures consistency across different platforms and devices. This improves audience retention and engagement, as viewers are less likely to switch away due to frustration from disorganized channel listings.




Media Asset Management (MAM)

Our MAM solution provides a centralized, scalable, and intelligent platform for organizing, storing, retrieving, and distributing media assets seamlessly. Our solution provides automated tools for organizing, storing, and retrieving content efficiently, ensuring seamless access and secure management across various storage environments. It supports ingestion, transcoding, and integration with playout systems, streamlining the entire content lifecycle from acquisition to distribution.

Remote Playout

Our remote playout services enable broadcasters to manage their channels from anywhere in the world. We offer fully automated and cloud-based playout solutions that provide:
• 24/7 monitoring & scheduling
• Dynamic content management
• Seamless integration with automation systems
• With our low-latency playout infrastructure, we ensure uninterrupted content delivery while reducing operational costs and complexities.

Contribution

We offer a comprehensive solution for high-quality DTH content delivery through Zixi, SRT, and Microwave transmission. Our solutions ensure secure, low-latency video transport with advanced error correction, guaranteeing uninterrupted and high-quality broadcasting. With these technologies, we deliver a reliable and efficient broadcasting solution tailored to meet industry demands.

Channel Uplink

We provide reliable and secure channel uplink services to ensure seamless transmission of TV channels to satellites. Our state-of-the-art teleport facilities support C-band and Ku-band uplinks, offering extensive coverage for global and regional broadcasting. Whether for free-to-air (FTA) or encrypted channels, our robust uplink solutions guarantee high availability, minimal latency, and superior signal quality.

Quality of Experience (QoE)

We prioritize innovation and technical excellence to ensure that video content is not only processed and distributed through our broadcast platforms but also delivers an exceptional viewing experience. Through proactive monitoring, in-depth performance analysis, and advanced mitigation strategies, we maintain the highest quality standards, optimizing service reliability for both end-users and audiences.
