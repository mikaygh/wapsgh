About WAPS

West Africa Platform Services (WAPS), a wholly owned subsidiary of K-NET was established in 2014. The initiative aims to deliver high-quality free-to-air (FTA) and free-to-view (FTV) channels to millions of viewers in the region, leveraging K-NET's state-of-the-art teleport facilities and expertise.
