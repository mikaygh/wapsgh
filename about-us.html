About WAPS

West Africa Platform Services (WAPS), a subsidiary of K-NET was established through a strategic partnership with SES in 2016. This collaboration aimed to enhance Direct-to-Home (DTH) broadcasting services across West and Central Africa by leveraging SES's satellite capacity and K-NET's teleport services. The joint venture sought to deliver high-quality free-to-air (FTA) and free-to-view (FTV) channels to millions of viewers in the region, utilizing K-NET's state-of-the-art teleport facillities and SES's prime orbital position at 28.2 degrees East.
